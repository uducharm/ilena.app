Features

Tools built for the creative process

Lexia supports every stage of writing — from first draft to final polish — with intelligent analysis and suggestions that stay true to your voice.

✍️

Lyric Editing & Storage

Write and organise all your songs in one place. Your lyrics are stored securely and accessible anywhere.

📖

Definition Dictionary

Look up any word without leaving your song. Understand nuance and pick the word that means exactly what you intend.

🎵

Rhyme Suggestions

Find the perfect rhyme — near, perfect, or slant — to keep your flow without compromising meaning.

📐

Rhythm & Syllable Analysis

Visualise syllable counts and stress patterns line by line to keep your metre consistent.

💡

Synonym & Antonym Tool

Explore alternatives for any word to sharpen imagery, avoid repetition, or find a better fit.

✨

Style Enhancement

Get AI-powered feedback on tone, imagery, and structure — insights that elevate your craft.

How it works

Write. Analyse. Refine.

Lexia slots into your existing creative workflow in three simple steps.

01

Write your draft

Open a new song and start writing. No templates, no constraints — just a blank canvas for your ideas.

02

Get instant feedback

Lexia analyses your lyrics in real time, flagging rhythm issues and suggesting rhymes and definitions as you go.

03

Refine with suggestions

Browse rhyme alternatives, synonyms, and style tips. Accept what fits, ignore what doesn't — you're always in control.

04

Save & revisit

Your songs are safely stored in the cloud. Pick up where you left off from any device, any time.
